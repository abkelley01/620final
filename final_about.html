About Us

Book Club Database was an idea formed originally to deal with the separation of a group of friends during the COVID-19 pandemic. Weekly trivia got a little stale and all of us were scattered across the country due to jobs, family, etc. In our griend group, one of our main passions has always been reading. So what better way to connect than create a book club? The first hurdle was picking a genre, since all of us are interested in a variety of topics. When we settled on a few genres to pull from, we realized that this might be an issue for many groups. Some like horror, some like historical fiction, and some only read non-fiction. So how did we solve this problem? We decided to expand our reading circles to include anyone who was interested. And so Book Club Database was brought to life.

Our main goal is to provide inspiration on what to read and people to connect with over those books that we have tackled already. Whether new to a book club or a veteran, we wanted to provide a welcoming, warm place for everyone to call home. This is our home for that.
